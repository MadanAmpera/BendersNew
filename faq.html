Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

What types of tours do you offer?

Benders Bus Tours offers a variety of wine tours in South Australia, including trips to the Barossa Valley, McLaren Vale, Clare Valley, and Adelaide Hills. We specialize in private tours and customized itineraries to provide a personalized experience. Our tours highlight the best wineries, scenic views, and gourmet food stops in each region.

What is the minimum or maximum group size you take?

We take groups ranging from a minimum of 4 people to a maximum of 40 people. Larger groups can also be accommodated and catered to. Get in touch with us to find out more.

How can I book a tour?

You can book a tour directly through our website or by contacting us via phone or email. Click on our “Book a tour’ tab available on every page. Send your query, and we will get in touch with you within a day.

Pick-Up and Drop-Off

Where are the pick-up and drop-off locations?

We offer complimentary pick-up and drop-off within a 15-kilometer radius of the Adelaide CBD. This includes popular spots such as hotels, train stations, landmarks, and the address of your choice. Additional pick-up locations can be arranged for a fee. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements for a seamless and convenient experience.

Can I request multiple pick-up locations?

Yes, you can request multiple pick-up locations. For additional pick-ups, depending on the distance between them, a fee of $50 to $100 will apply. This ensures everyone in your group can join the tour without hassle.

Wine Tour Details

Which wine regions do you cover?

We cover the most renowned wine regions in South Australia, including the Barossa Valley, McLaren Vale, Clare Valley, and Adelaide Hills. Each region offers unique experiences and exceptional wines, from bold reds to crisp whites, providing an unforgettable wine-tasting adventure.

What is included in a typical wine tour?

Our wine tours typically include visits to 3 wineries, wine tastings, and a lunch stop. Each tour is designed to provide a comprehensive and enjoyable wine-tasting experience. We select top-rated wineries to ensure you sample the best wines the region has to offer.

Can I customize my wine tour itinerary?

Absolutely! We offer tailor-made tours to suit your preferences. Whether you want to visit specific wineries, include additional activities, or focus on a particular type of wine, we can create a custom itinerary for you. Personalized tours ensure you get the most out of your wine-tasting experience.

Pricing and Payments

How much does a wine tour cost?

The cost of our wine tours varies depending on the tour type and the number of participants. Please visit our ‘Tours’ section for pricing details for each wine region. We offer competitive rates to ensure you receive excellent value for your wine tour experience.

Are tasting fees included in the tour price?

Tasting fees are typically included in the tour price, but some wineries may charge additional fees. We will inform you of any extra charges when we finalize your itinerary. This transparency ensures no surprises on your tour day.

Special Requests and Accommodations

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate dietary restrictions. Please inform us of any special requirements when you book your tour, and we will make the necessary arrangements. Ensuring all guests have a comfortable and enjoyable experience is our priority.

Can I bring children on the tour?

Children are welcome on our private tours. Please note that wine tasting is restricted to participants over the age of 18. Family-friendly options are available to ensure everyone enjoys the day.

Is the tour vehicle accessible for people with disabilities?

We strive to accommodate all guests. Please contact us to discuss any specific accessibility needs, and we will do our best to make suitable arrangements. Accessibility is important to us, and we aim to provide an inclusive experience for all.

COVID-19 Policies

What safety measures are in place due to COVID-19?

We adhere to all local health guidelines to ensure the safety of our guests. This includes regular sanitation of vehicles, providing hand sanitizer, and practicing social distancing during tours. Your health and safety are our top priority.

You can contact us via email benderbustour@gmail.com or call us at 0481 313 409 or 0434 077 017. For more information, visit our ‘Get in Touch’ page. We are ready to assist you with any queries or special requests.
