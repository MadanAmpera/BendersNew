About McLaren Vale

Just 45 minutes south of Adelaide lies McLaren Vale, one of Australia’s oldest and most celebrated wine regions—a true haven for wine enthusiasts and foodies alike. With over 80 vineyards and incredible culinary delights at every turn, McLaren Vale is perfect for those looking to eat and drink their way through iconic wineries. Nestled near the stunning coastline and sandy beaches of the Fleurieu Peninsula, this region offers more than just wine. Let Bender’s Bus Tours take you on a memorable day trip, where relaxation and indulgence go hand in hand.

SO MANY WORLD-CLASS VARIETALS TO ENJOY ON YOUR ADELAIDE GROUP WINE TOUR. While McLaren Vale is famous for its Shiraz, it also excels in producing premium Grenache and Cabernet. Mediterranean varieties like Fiano, Vermentino, Tempranillo, and Sangiovese thrive in the region’s climate, offering even more layers of discovery for wine lovers.

Many cellar doors also feature local produce, elevating your tasting experience. From gourmet dining to casual platters, there’s a perfect wine and food pairing for every palate.
