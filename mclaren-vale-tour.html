What to expect

  1. The cost for Barossa Valley Wine Tour one is from $115.00 per person. Includes lunch and standard wine tasting fees unless otherwise stated for the above listed tour.
  2. These prices are based on a minimum number of people attending on tour day. ( Minimum 8 people for the 13 seat bus or minimum 14 people for the 20 seat bus )
  3. If you wish to customise your very own tour, please let us know and we will organise the whole day for you.
  4. All standard wine tastings included in your tour price, unless otherwise stated.
  5. If any suggested wineries are not available then other wineries are included but any tasting fees applicable will be at your own cost.
  6. All wineries / breweries and venues are subject to availability and group size limits.
  7. We can also arrange a different location for the pickup if the CBD is not required. Some charges may apply.
