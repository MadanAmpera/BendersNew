Discover Barossa

Swap the hustle and bustle of Adelaide for a relaxing day in the world-famous Barossa Valley.

This guided small-group tour combines premium wine tastings, gourmet food, and a taste of the Barossa lifestyle. From boutique wineries to indulgent chocolates and Maggie Beer’s iconic farm shop, every stop is designed to create a memorable experience.

Tour Highlights

  • Visit 4 premium wineries for tastings

  • Enjoy a gourmet lunch at a Barossa winery

  • Indulge in local treats – donuts, coffee, chocolate & Maggie Beer’s Farm Shop

  • Take a leisurely walk through Tanunda town

  • Small-group experience with a maximum of 24 travellers

  • Free pick-up & drop-off within 10 km of Adelaide CBD

What's included

  • 4 wine tastings (admission included)

  • Gourmet lunch

  • Transportation in a comfortable, air-conditioned bus

  • Bottle of water for every guest

  • Experienced English-speaking guides.

Important Information

  • Duration: Approx. 8 hours

  • Ages: 0–80

  • Group size: Maximum 24 travellers

  • Confirmation received at time of booking

  • Not recommended for pregnant travellers or those with back problems

  • Due to high demand, venues may be substituted with similar locations to ensure a quality experience

What to expect

Itinerary

Itinerary:

Pick-up

  • From Adelaide hotels (within 10 km) or meeting point at Stamford Plaza or contact us for any arrangements to be made for your pickups outside the radius.

Morning

  • 10:00 am – Chateau Yaldara

    40 min premium wine tasting (admission included)

  • 11:00 am – Browns Barossa Donuts

    Quick 5 min sweet stop

  • 11:15 am – Darling’s Food With Passion Café

    Short 5 min coffee break

  • 11:30 am – Tanunda Town Walk

    30 min guided stroll through the charming Barossa town (admission included)

Midday

  • 12:15 pm – Langmeil Winery

    45 min wine tasting (admission included)

  • 1:15 pm – Hemera Estate

    45 min wine tasting (admission included)

  • 2:00 pm – Kies Family Wines

    90 min wine tasting & gourmet lunch (admission included)

    Scenic drive past Jacob’s Creek Trail of Discovery en route

Afternoon

  • 3:45 pm – Barossa Valley Chocolate Company

    10 min indulgent chocolate stop

  • 4:00 pm – Maggie Beer’s Farm Shop

    25 min visit to the Barossa icon

  • 4:45 pm – Chateau Dorrien

    40 min wine tasting (admission included)

  • 5:30 pm – Ubertas Wines

    40 min wine tasting (admission included)

    Scenic return drive via Adelaide city

Drop-off

  • Return to your Adelaide accommodation
