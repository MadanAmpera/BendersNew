Hahndorf and Barossa

Discover the best of Hahndorf and the Barossa Valley in one unforgettable day. Begin with a walk through Hahndorf’s charming village streets, rich in German heritage, then enjoy strawberry picking at Beerenberg Farm. Journey into the world-renowned Barossa Valley for two boutique wine tastings at Kies Family Wines and Château Dorrien, both celebrated for their welcoming atmosphere and exceptional wines. Savour a gourmet lunch showcasing regional flavours, followed by a sweet stop at the Barossa Valley Chocolate Company for handcrafted chocolates and gelato. End the day at Maggie Beer’s Farm Shop, where you can sample local delights and relax by the scenic pond. This tour blends culture, wine, fresh produce, and indulgence—offering exceptional value and lasting memories.

Tour Details:

  • Ages: 0-80

  • Group Limit: 13

  • Duration: 8h

  • Live guide: English

What to expect

Itinerary

Pass By: Adelaide Hills, Adelaide Hills, Greater Adelaide, South Australia

Begin your journey with a picturesque drive from Adelaide City through the stunning Adelaide Hills. This scenic route is dotted with rolling vineyards, lush greenery, and charming countryside views. Along the way, you'll pass through quaint villages and enjoy glimpses of South Australia's natural beauty, setting the tone for a day of exploration and indulgence. The drive to Hahndorf offers a tranquil escape from the city, making it a perfect start to your adventure.

Stop At: Hahndorf, Hahndorf, Adelaide Hills, Greater Adelaide, South Australia

Kick off your adventure in the historic village of Hahndorf, Australia’s oldest German settlement. Explore its charming streets, filled with unique boutiques, artisan shops, and delightful cafes. Savor the authentic atmosphere and perhaps enjoy a traditional German pastry.

Duration: 1 hour 15 minutes

Stop At: Beerenberg Farm, 2106 Mount Barker Rd, Hahndorf SA 5245, Australia

Next, visit Beerenberg Farm, a haven for fresh, seasonal strawberries. Participate in strawberry picking (seasonal activity), with the entry fee included in your tour. Please note that strawberries picked are charged by weight at $12.50 per kilogram, payable directly at the farm. After picking, explore their farm shop offering a range of homemade jams, sauces, and condiments.

Duration: 45 minutes

Stop At: Melba's Chocolate & Confectionery, 22 Henry St, Woodside, South Australia 5244 Australia

A stop at Melba’s Chocolates in Woodside is always a delight. Set inside an old cheese factory, Melba’s has transformed the space into a charming working confectionery where you can see traditional chocolate- and lolly-making equipment in action. The experience is hands-on in the sense that you can wander through the different production areas, watch the chocolatiers at work, and of course, taste and shop your way through a huge range of handmade treats. From classic chocolate-coated honeycomb to nostalgic boiled sweets, there’s something to tempt every sweet tooth. It’s a favourite stop for families and visitors looking to take home a little taste of the Adelaide Hills.

Duration: 20 minutes

Pass By: Barossa Valley, Barossa Valley, South Australia

The journey from Hahndorf to the Barossa Valley takes you through the heart of the Adelaide Hills, offering breathtaking views of rolling vineyards, dense forests, and charming farmlands. This scenic drive is not only a feast for the eyes but also a chance to spot local wildlife such as kangaroos, koalas, and colorful bird species in their natural habitat. The ever-changing landscape, dotted with rustic cottages and pristine valleys, creates the perfect backdrop for a tranquil and memorable drive to South Australia's renowned wine region.

Stop At: Kies Family Wines, 1303 Barossa Valley Way, Lyndoch, South Australia 5351 Australia

Upon reaching Barossa enjoy lunch and a wine tasting at Kies Family Wines, a family-owned gem in the Barossa Valley. Relish a gourmet meal at the Monkey Nut Café, offering vineyard views and local flavors. After lunch, indulge in a complimentary wine-tasting session at their historic ironstone cottage cellar door, where knowledgeable staff will guide you through a selection of premium wines.

Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Pass By: Tanunda, Tanunda, Barossa Valley, South Australia

As you pass through the picturesque town of Tanunda in the heart of the Barossa Valley, you'll be captivated by its charm and character. Known for its rich German heritage, Tanunda features historic buildings, tree-lined streets, and welcoming boutique shops. The town is surrounded by rolling vineyards and wineries, making it a quintessential Barossa Valley experience. If time permits, a short stop allows you to explore its quaint cafes, local artisan stores, and perhaps capture a photo of the stunning landscape that embraces this vibrant community.

Stop At: Maggie Beer's Farm Shop, Pheasant Farm Road, Nuriootpa, South Australia 5355 Australia

Wrap up your journey with a visit to Maggie Beer’s Farm Shop, where you can sample a variety of her gourmet products. Relax by the scenic pond and perhaps spot some local wildlife while enjoying the tranquil atmosphere.

Duration: 20 minutes

Stop At: Chateau Dorrien, Cnr. Barossa Valley Way & Seppeltsfield Rd Crn Barossa Valley Way and Seppeltsfield Road, Tanunda, South Australia 5352 Australia

As the day winds down in the Barossa Valley, we make one final, unforgettable stop. Nestled among the vines, Chateau Dorrien Winery welcomes us with its rustic charm and rich heritage. Here, guests are invited to slow down and savour a relaxed tasting of elegant reds, crisp whites, and the winery’s specialty—smooth, velvety ports. Surrounded by scenic vineyard views, it’s the perfect setting to reflect on the day’s discoveries with a glass in hand.

Or, if the day leads us to Ubertas Wines, you’ll enjoy a boutique experience with a modern twist. This family-run winery is known for its sustainable, low-intervention winemaking and its warm, down-to-earth hospitality. Overlooking pristine vineyards, their cellar door offers a serene space to enjoy bold Shiraz, vibrant whites, and more—all crafted with care and character.

Whether at Dorrien or Ubertas, this final tasting is not just about wine—it’s about ending your Barossa journey on a high note, where every sip tells a story.

Duration: 45 minutes

Stop At: Ubertas Wines, 790 Research Rd Light Pass, Nuriootpa, South Australia 5355 Australia

A visit to Ubertas Wines is a chance to experience the passion of a boutique, family-owned winery in the heart of the Barossa Valley. Founded by two brothers who brought their vision from Asia to Australia, Ubertas is all about combining tradition with innovation. Guests are welcomed into a relaxed and contemporary cellar door where they can enjoy thoughtfully crafted wines, each reflecting the rich character of the Barossa. From elegant reds to crisp whites, the tastings here are personal and engaging, often guided by the winemakers themselves. It’s a stop that not only showcases quality wine but also tells an inspiring story of family, dedication, and the love of winemaking.

Duration: 45 minutes
