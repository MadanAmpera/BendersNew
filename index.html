Wine Tasting Tour Adelaide

The Best Of  Our Adelaide Wine Tours

Time and time again, South Australia’s wine regions have topped the ‘best wine regions in Australia’ lists. Adelaide Hills, Barossa Valley, and McLaren Vale are uniquely positioned and are some of the only regions that can take advantage of both warm and cool climate growing conditions. When you combine that with South Australia’s passion for pairing a drop with exquisite meals, it’s not hard to understand why tourists flock to the region to do wine tasting in Adelaide. Benders Bus Tours have been operating in the area for a while, and we’re favourites in the region for our superior service, our incredible wine tours, and our fantastic value for money. Here are a few of the highlights from each of our tours to give you an idea of what to expect when you book Benders Bus Tours for wine tasting in Adelaide.

Wine Tasting In Adelaide Hills

Our Adelaide Hills wine tour is second to none when it comes to sightseeing, incredible food, and wine tasting. Whether you're bringing a small group of close friends or you have a whole crew of excited wine enthusiasts, you're guaranteed to love this tour.

  • Bird In Hand winery. The first stop on the Adelaide Hills tour is the Bird In Hand winery. This winery is renowned in the area for their shiraz, but they also make beautiful pinot noir, chardonnay, and riesling amongst others. Bird In Hand has a very earthy, nature-rich atmosphere, making it the perfect first stop for the day.

  • Shopping in Hahndorf. If you adore German architecture, food, and history, you are going to fall in love with Hahndorf. As part of wine tasting in Adelaide Hills, we include a stop into the historic town so you can shop artisanal products to your heart's content.

  • Mini-golf at Sidewood Wines. After a long day of eating, drinking, and shopping, the perfect way to let your hair down is a round of mini-golf at Sidewood Wines. Don't expect your aim to be too fantastic!

Wine Tasting In Barossa Valley

There are so many opportunities for wine tasting in Barossa Valley; we simply couldn't put it all into one tour. You'll simply have to come back and do a second round with Bender Bus Tours if you want to experience all of the Barossa Valley. There are a couple of places, however, that are incredible enough that we had to include them on both tours.

  • Langmeil Winery. The Freedom 1843 is believed to be the oldest Shiraz vineyard in the world, and you can find it at Langmeil Winery in the Barossa Valley. You'll have the opportunity to experience the rich history of this incredible winery and sample some of the finest wines in Australia.

  • Kies Family Wines. Kies Family Wines are just as renowned for their food and cafe as they are for their incredible wines. Both our Barossa Valley tours have the option to stop in at this family-owned winery for a bite to eat and to sample their vast range of vintages.

  • Woodstock Winery. If you're looking for a wine that is unashamedly Australian in origin and captures the essence of McLaren Vale, you can't go past the vintages on offer at Woodstock Winery. Visiting this vineyard is a truly wonderful experience, which is why we offer it on both of our tours wine tasting in McLaren Vale.

Wine Tasting In McLaren Vale

You can't do wine tasting in Adelaide without visiting the McLaren Vale region. Much like our Barossa Valley tours, we couldn't narrow down all the marvellous wineries, cafes, and shops into one tour, so we've spread them out over two. There are a couple of destinations, however, that make appearances on both tours.

  • Australian Boutique Premium Wines. This little winery has been in operation for more than 25 years, and they specialise in connecting small wine producers to customers. If you're looking for unique wines you wouldn't find anywhere else, Australian Boutique Premium Wines is the place for you.

  • Menz chocolate shop. When you choose Benders Bus Tours for wine tasting in McLaren Vale, you'll end the day in Menz chocolate shop. The boutique, handmade chocolates are infamous in the area, and a sweet treat is just the thing to finish off a long day of sampling decadent wines.

  • Woodstock Winery. If you're looking for a wine that is unashamedly Australian in origin and captures the essence of McLaren Vale, you can't go past the vintages on offer at Woodstock Winery. Visiting this vineyard is a truly wonderful experience, which is why we offer it on both of our tours wine tasting in McLaren Vale.
